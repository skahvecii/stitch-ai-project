Cookies Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2023

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) to help websites function properly and provide a better user experience. For PriceRadar, they allow us to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and analyze how you interact with our platform to improve price comparison accuracy.

How we use them

PriceRadar uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience in several ways:

  • check_circle Authenticating your account and keeping you signed in.
  • check_circle Remembering your local currency and language settings.
  • check_circle Tracking your "watched" items to provide real-time price drop alerts.
  • check_circle Analyzing traffic patterns to optimize site performance.

Affiliate Tracking Cookies

PriceRadar is a free comparison tool. We earn a small commission from some retailers when you click through our links and make a purchase. This is made possible through Affiliate Tracking Cookies.

These cookies do not cost you anything extra. They simply tell the retailer that you came from PriceRadar so we can be credited for the referral. This revenue allows us to maintain the platform and keep our comparison data 100% unbiased.

Third-party cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-parties cookies to report usage statistics of the service, deliver advertisements on and through the service, and so on. These third parties include Google Analytics for usage tracking and specialized marketing partners for personalized deal recommendations.

Managing your preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website though your access to some functionality and areas of our website may be restricted.
