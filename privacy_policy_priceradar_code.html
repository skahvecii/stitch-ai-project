1. Introduction

Welcome to PriceRadar. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our price comparison services.

By using PriceRadar, you consent to the data practices described in this statement. We are committed to protecting your personal data and providing a secure environment for price tracking and deal discovery.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address, name, and profile preferences.
  • Tracking Data: Products you watch, price alerts you set, and stores you follow.
  • Log Information: We automatically collect device information, IP address, browser type, and interaction data.
  • Communication: Records of correspondence if you contact our support team.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service, including price tracking alerts.
  • To notify you about changes to our service or new deals.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our service.
  • To monitor the usage of our service and detect technical issues.

4. Cookies and Tracking

cookie

Cookie Usage Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

5. Affiliate Disclosure

PriceRadar is a price comparison platform. We want to be transparent about how we keep our service free for users.

When you click on links to various merchants on this site and make a purchase, this can result in this site earning a commission. Affiliate programs and affiliations include, but are not limited to, the eBay Partner Network, Amazon Associates, and Rakuten Advertising.

This does not affect the prices you see or the neutrality of our price comparisons. Our primary goal is always to find you the best price regardless of affiliate status.

6. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption for all data transfers and secure hashing for passwords, to protect your personal information against unauthorized access or disclosure.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data under laws like GDPR or CCPA:

  • Right to Access: You can request copies of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request that we erase your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data.
